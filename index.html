Smart Plant Monitoring System

About Our Project

Agriculture plays an important role in the development of agricultural countries. Some problems affecting agriculture have always hindered the development of the country. Consequently, the only solution to this problem is smart agriculture by modernizing the current traditional methods of agriculture. We have proposed ESP8266 IoT Automatic Irrigation System to modernize and improve crop productivity. We used Soil Moisture Sensor to measure the moisture content in the soil. Similarly, we used DHT11 Humidity & Temperature Sensor to measure Air Temperature and humidity. You can also monitor all this happening remotely via Thingspeak Server online from any part of the world.
